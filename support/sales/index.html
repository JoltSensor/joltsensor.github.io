Sales & Purchasing

Where to Buy

Right now the Jolt Sensor is sold exclusively through the the Jolt website at joltsensor.com. We have plans to expand to additional online retail outlets, as well as local sports specialty stores before making the jump to national retail chains. As our distribution expands, we'll post updates on all the places you can find Jolt Sensor.

How to Place an Order

There's an "Order Now" button at the upper right of every page on the Jolt website that links to the shop page. There you can find product details and specifications and simply press the "Add to Cart" button to start an order. A checkout overlay will pop up, where you can complete the rest of the order process. If you have any issues with ordering, please send a message to support@joltsensor.com.

Group Discounts

There are group discounts available if you're looking to outfit a team, league, or other large organization. Discounts start at 15% and increase with your group's size. Send us a message at sales@joltsensor.com, and we'll send you a discount code to pass along to your group members. Direct bulk orders are also available.

Distribution Inquiries

Interested in becoming an official Jolt distributor? Send us a message at distribution@joltsensor.com to kick off the conversation.
