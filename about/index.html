Our Story

Jolt was born out of necessity. During his junior year at MIT, founder Ben Harvatine suffered a nasty concussion at wrestling practice. It went unnoticed until he had suffered additional impacts, resulting in a much more severe injury. Following a long painful recovery process, he wired up his wrestling headgear with sensors for an engineering lab class and headed back to the mat determined to develop a solution that would prevent others from having the same experience that he did.

Our Mission

Jolt is on a mission to help athletes train smarter and safer through innovative technology solutions. We aim to provide highly-functional and intuitive products that provide athletes, parents, and coaches real-time feedback, and enable them to make smarter more timely decisions.
