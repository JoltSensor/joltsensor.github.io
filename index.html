PLAY SMARTER

Get live head impact data and real-time alerts in the palm of your hand with the Jolt Sensor

A Solution For Every Sport

Clip the sensor to whatever you wear on your head. Whether you wear a sweatband, helmet, hairband, baseball cap, or wrestling headgear, the sensor can quickly and easily detach and re-mount for seamless transitions between sports.

Live Data and Alerts

The sensor is in constant communication with the app, with a range of over 200 yards. When a significant impact is detected, the sensor vibrates to alert the player, while the mobile app alerts parents and coaches that the athlete should be removed from play for an evaluation.

Guided Recovery

The app captures symptoms identified in sideline evaluation, as well as the return to play decision. It automatically follows up after a few days to see if a concussion has been diagnosed by a doctor, and then follows up daily to track symptoms and guide recovery.

Any Athlete, Any Sport

Concussions can happen to any athlete in any sport. That's why the Jolt Sensor works for ALL of them.
