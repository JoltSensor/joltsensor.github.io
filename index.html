It's Time to Play Smarter

Real-time head impact tracking for athletes. Peace of mind for parents and coaches.

1. WEAR

Clip the Jolt Sensor to any type of headgear, whether a headband, helmet, or anything in between.

2. MONITOR

All nearby connected smartphones will see the frequency and severity of head impacts in real time.

3. EVALUATE

When a significant impact is detected, capture the athlete's sideline assessment directly in the app.

EVERY ATHLETE IS AT RISK

At least 3.1 million school-aged athletes will be diagnosed with concussions this year. But considering up to 80% of concussions go undiagnosed, the real number is staggeringly higher. The truth is, most of the time, players don't know they've taken a bad hit. While no sports product can prevent a concussion, Jolt solves the biggest problem we face with sports concussions - you can't treat or look for symptoms if you don't know a player has had an impact. Now you can, with JoltSensor.
