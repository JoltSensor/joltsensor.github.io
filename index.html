The Jolt Sensor

The Jolt Sensor is a wearable multi-sport head impact sensor. The sensor provides immediate feedback to athletes, keeping them aware of head impacts as they happen. And the connected mobile app enables parents, coaches, and trainers to track their athletes' impacts in real time.

  • Works with existing equipment, from headbands to helmets
  • Streams data and alerts to your phone using Bluetooth low energy
  • Rechargeable battery lasts weeks on a single charge
