Jolt is a wearable impact sensor for youth athletes. It allows parents and coaches to track athlete's head impacts in real-time and enables them to act decisively in the critical moments following a dangerous impact.

Mount the sensor to whatever you wear on your head. Whether it's a sweatband, hairband, baseball cap, wrestling headgear, or helmet, it'll work. Jolt's versatile patent-pending design means one sensor for all of your activities that you can quickly and easily detach and re-mount for seamless transitions between activities.
